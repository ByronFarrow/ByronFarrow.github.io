Experience

Completing my education in Physics with Astrophysics at Birmingham in 1995 I then studied for my PGCE in Secondary Education, also at Birmingham. I then worked at schools in the UK, Hungary and Colombia before moving to China in 2006.

As well as teaching Physics and Theory of Knowledge (an IB-specific course related to philosophy and epistemology) I worked as a curriculum coordinator and as a grade-level coordinator / head of year. I have also worked directly for the IB in the roles of school visitor, consultant and exam author.
